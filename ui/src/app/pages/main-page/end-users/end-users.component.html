For End-Users

The entry point of the EOSC Core Innovation Sandbox is the EOSC Beyond User Space, the user interface that allows you to navigate through the EOSC Federated Catalogue, access and deploy EOSC services and resources.

As a user in the EOSC Core Innovation Sandbox, you can:

Login with your institutional credentials
Discover services, datasets, and other research products
Order a service
Download or transfer datasets
Check the quality of a service through the EOSC Monitoring service
Check the usage of a service through the EOSC Accounting service
